Peanut Butter Cookies

There's nothing like a peanut butter cookie to satisfy your sweet tooth and lift your spirits. This peanut butter cookie recipe will quickly become a family favorite.

This easy recipe can be made in 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Fine Sea Salt
  • 1 Cup Room Temp Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 Cup Light Brown Sugar
  • 1 Cup Smooth Peanut Butter
  • 2 Large Room Temp eggs
  • 2 Cups Peanut Butter Chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375ÂºF
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt
  3. In a mixer, beat the butter, granulated sugar, peanut butter and brown sugar until creamy
  4. Add the eggs, then gradually beat in the dry ingredient mixture, finally stir in the peanut butter chips
  5. Wrap the completed dough and chill for 1 hour
  6. Divide the dough into 3-tablespoon sized balls using a large cookie scoop and drop onto baking sheets 2 inches apart
  7. Bake for 11~13 minutes, or until golden brown
  8. Cool for 5 minutes before moving the cookies to wire racks
  9. Enjoy!
