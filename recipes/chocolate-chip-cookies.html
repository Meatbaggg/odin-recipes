Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ultra thick bakery style chocolate chip cookies feature golden brown edges with ooey and gooey centers.

This easy recipe can be made in 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 Teaspoon Fine Sea Salt
  • 2 Sticks Room Temp Unsalted Butter
  • 1/2 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups Light Brown Sugar
  • 2 Teaspoons Vanilla
  • 2 Large Room Temp eggs
  • 2 Cups Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350ÂºF
  2. In a medium bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, and salt
  3. In a mixer, beat the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until creamy
  4. Add the vanilla and eggs, then gradually beat in the dry ingredient mixture, finally stir in the chocolate chips
  5. Wrap the completed dough and refrigerate for 24 hours
  6. Divide the dough into 3-tablespoon sized balls using a large cookie scoop and drop onto baking sheets lined with parchment paper
  7. Bake for 11~13 minutes, or until golden brown
  8. Cool for 5 minutes before moving the cookies to wire racks
  9. Enjoy!
